Brand Guide

Core visual guidelines for Velocè Logistics.

Brand Colors

  • Primary: #0A2742
  • Secondary: #8FA3BF
  • Accent: #F4F4F6
  • Maroon: #AA1F34
  • Maroon Dark: #470500
  • Charcoal: #222A2A
  • White: #FFFFFF

The Velocè Logistics brand combines a deep maroon with structured, professional blues and neutral accents. Maroon tones emphasize speed and urgency, while the primary blue and charcoal convey trust and reliability.

Typography

The website uses system-friendly fonts for performance and clarity:

  • Headlines: Bold, uppercase or title-case using Arial / Helvetica.
  • Body: Regular weight, high contrast against white or light accent backgrounds.

Logo Usage

The Velocè Logistics logo should be used on a white or very light background whenever possible. Maintain clear space around the logo, and avoid stretching, distorting, or recoloring the core mark.
