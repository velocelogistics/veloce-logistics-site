Velocè Logistics Brand Guide

Your complete reference for using Velocè branding consistently across all media.

Logo Usage

The Velocè logo consists of the stylized Speedlines Wordmark and the Winged V icon.

  • Use navy or maroon versions only.
  • Maintain clear_space equal to the height of the V.
  • Do not stretch, rotate, warp, or recolor the logo.

Files Provided: PNG, SVG, transparent variants.

Typography

Velocè uses clean, modern sans-serif fonts.

  • Headlines: Bold, Title Case
  • Body Text: Regular, 1.5 line height
  • Buttons: Bold, short labels

Logo Do’s & Don’ts

  • ✔ Use provided colors only
  • ✔ Keep clear space
  • ✖ No stretched or distorted logos
  • ✖ No unapproved color changes
  • ✖ No placing logo on cluttered backgrounds

Brand Voice

The tone is confident, professional, and clear.

  • Speak directly and simply
  • Highlight reliability and speed
  • Avoid exaggerations or gimmicks
