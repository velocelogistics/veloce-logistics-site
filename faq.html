Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about partnering with Velocè Logistics.

General Questions

What types of shipments do you handle?

We primarily support parcel-sized shipments for last-mile delivery, route coverage, and regional runs. Larger freight or special handling can be discussed on a case-by-case basis.

Do you work directly with shippers or only with carriers?

Our primary partnerships are with national and regional carriers, but we also support select direct shipper and business-to-business customers.

How do we get a quote?

Use our Request a Quote page to share your route, volume, and service needs, and we’ll follow up with a tailored proposal.

Operations & Coverage

What areas do you cover?

Our coverage is focused on specific metro and regional zones. Visit our Coverage page or contact us to discuss your location.

Can you support seasonal or peak volume?

Yes. We routinely plan for peak and seasonal surges, and can help you build a coverage model that flexes with your volume.
